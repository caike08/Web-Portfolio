About me

Hey, I'm Carlos Henrique, also known as Caike. I am a software engineering student from Universidade do Vale do Sapucaí based in Brazil who has taken part of an exchange program at Eötvös Loránd University in Budapest, Hungary.

Web Development is, for sure, one of my biggest passions. As well as a web developer, I am also a bass and guitar player.

I have worked as front-end developer at B2ML Sistemas, working with CSS3, HTML, JavaScript and a bit of PHP. I also worked as a Protheus 10 instructor at TOTVS for over a year. During my exchange program, I took part in a project group which was responsible for building an educational platform, based on Canvas, to help professors and students share content in academia.
