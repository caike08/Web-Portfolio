About me

Hey, I'm Carlos Henrique, also known as Caike. I am a software engineering student from Universidade do Vale do Sapucaí based in Brazil who has taken part of an exchange program at Eötvös Loránd University in Budapest, Hungary.

I'm a person who shares his passion for technology and music. I believe that creativity and application are both a key to success. Turn ideas into reality is one of the most grateful things you can make, and it's pretty motivating to work with such things :)

My last job was at Amagafa TECH, where I worked as a front-end developer, allowing me to work with HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, JS and GruntJS. Furthermore, I worked as a front-end development at B2ML Sistemas, using CSS, HTML, JavaScript and a bit of PHP. I also had the chance to work as a Protheus trainer at TOTVS for over a year.

Last but certainly not least, I had the chance to study in one of the greatest universities in Budapest, ELTE. During my exchange program, I took part in a project group which was responsible for building an educational platform, based in Canvas, to help professors and students share content in academia.
